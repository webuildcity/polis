What is OpenPlots?

OpenPlots is an open data-based platform for accessing information on concrete building projects up to three years in advance and information on location factors - facilitating decision-making for real estate developers, architects, urban planners and any stakeholder interested in real estate.

Who runs this platform?

WE-BUILD.CITY SERVICES UG (haftungsbeschränkt) is an aspiring company from Hamburg & Berlin (Germany) that seeks to develop innovative programs to support the public authority's work in relation to urban planning. As a software and service provider for the public authorities and the citizens, we provide digital solutions for making urban planning and administration processes more transparent for the citizens and more legitimate, on basis of our Open Source Platform We-build.city. We support public administrations and operators of local news platforms (e.g. local newspapers, blogs, etc.) in their work, making their communication process faster, more effective and easier.
